Patch frontend/src/pages/LeaderboardPage.jsx

1. Add import:

import LeaderboardOverview from '../components/leaderboard/LeaderboardOverview';

2. Find where LeaderboardTable is rendered.

Usually it looks like:

<LeaderboardTable
  items={items}
  totalItems={meta.total}
  selectedFilter={selectedFilter}
/>

3. Place this directly above LeaderboardTable:

<LeaderboardOverview items={items} />

Full target area should become approximately:

<LeaderboardOverview items={items} />

<LeaderboardTable
  items={items}
  totalItems={meta.total}
  selectedFilter={selectedFilter}
/>

If your page stores the response as data.items, use:

<LeaderboardOverview items={data.items || []} />
